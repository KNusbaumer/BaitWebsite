Community

We aim to be a one-stop shop for all of your fishing needs. Which includes needing a fishing license to properly (and legally!) enjoy your fishing trip!

Our establishment is an example of our community, so we always want to give back our appericate for you as the shopper to support local and small bussinesses!

We hold a famous tradition in our community called "Howard's Lakeside Annual Morel Mushroom Contest" which helps bring the community together and the winner always recives $100 giftcard from our bussiness in addition to a local artist's hand carved mushroom statue! (Which is different every year)

We are a close-knit community through and through and our social media outlets show that!
We encourage anyone and everyone who has caught a fish using any of our products to submit a photo of their catch! Wether the fish is big or small, wether youre old or young, we always want to know that our products are working for their indented use! To have a good fishing day! We love seeing those smiles!
