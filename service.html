Hunting & Fishing Licenses

We aim to be a one-stop stop for all of your fishing needs. Which includes needing a fishing license to properly (and legally!) enjoy your fishing trip!

We are one of many participating agents in the state of Ohio to help anyone in the state purchase a fishing or hunting license. The tables below show the mutiple options for each licensing option! Just stop on by to register and purchase your license on during our open hours!

Prices may vary on factors like residency and age.
If you have any questions or concerns with pre-existing licensing please contact Division of Wildlife at 1-800-WILDLIFE or email WildInfo@dnr.ohio.gov to recieve more infomation.

Respooling Service

We offer FREE and easy respooling service for your broken down reels!

Everyone has had to miss out on the perfect fishing-on-the-lake kinda day because of broken reels or line that is tangled beyond repair. We want to help you with that!

Just stop by to drop off your reels and line during working hours and let us know your preferred settings on your reels and give us a short time of 24 hours and you will be able to properly enjoy those spontanous fishing trips again!
